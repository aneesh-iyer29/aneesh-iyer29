Background

I competed in Science Olympiad for six years, starting with the Mason Middle School team and continuing on the High School team for four years. In my senior year, I was elected team captain and helped lead us to a 3rd place finish at the National Tournament. Over the years, I specialized in events such as Codebusters (cryptography), Optics, Experimental Design, and Bungee Drop, gaining hands-on experience across diverse areas of STEM.

Additionally, I volunteered to write tests for many invitational tournaments, with a comprehensive bank of the tests that I have written being found below and links (if publicly available).
